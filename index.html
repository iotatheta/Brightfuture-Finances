Investment Types

Explore different investment opportunities tailored to your risk profile:

  • Real Estate Investment

    Real estate investment involves purchasing, managing, and renting properties to generate a return.

  • Large Cap Investment

    Large Cap companies are well-established with strong performance, suitable for low-risk portfolios.

  • Mid Cap Investment

    Mid Cap companies offer growth potential with moderate risk, balancing stability and returns.

  • Small Cap Investment

    Small Cap companies are emerging businesses, offering high risk but greater growth potential.

  • Precious Metals Investment

    Invest in Gold, Silver, and other metals for a hedge against inflation and market instability.

  • Cryptocurrency Investment

    Invest in digital currencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum for high-risk, high-reward opportunities.

  • Mutual Fund

    Invest in Mutual Fund that allows investors to diversify across a board portfolio of stocks, bonds or other securities managed by professionals.

  • Startup

    A high-risk, high-reward investment in an emerging business, often characterized by innovation and the potential for rapid growth.

  • Art & Artifact

    A long-term investment in rare or unique pieces, with value driven by scarcity, artist reputation and marked trends, often appreciating over time.

Personal Finance Visualization

Structured plan for getting out of debt, building wealth and achieving financial stability :

  1. Save INR 1 lac (100000) for a Starter Emergency Fund :
    This is a financial safety net for minor emergencies.
  2. Pay Off All Debt (Except the House) Using the Debt Snowball :
    List your debts from smallest to largest and pay them off in that order, while making minimum payments on all debts except the smallest.
  3. Save 3 to 6 Months of Expenses in a Fully Funded Emergency Fund :
    Build a more substantial emergency fund to cover big life events like job loss or major repairs.
  4. Invest 15% of Your Household Income in Retirement :
    Start investing for your future in retirement accounts like elss mutual funds or national pension scheme and growth mutual funds which are tax saving.
  5. Save for Your Children's College Fund :
    Set aside funds for your children's education in tax-advantaged accounts like an ESA or 529 plan.
  6. Pay Off Your Home Early :
    Focus on paying off your mortgage early, which will give you more financial freedom.
  7. Build Wealth and Give :
    After completing the steps, continue to grow wealth through investments and live generously.

Investment and Future planning is subject to market risk so please read the documents carefully before investing
